Mr. Bilbo Baggins

“In every small heart lies the courage to face great adventures; it just takes a spark to light the way.”

Summary Bilbo Baggins lived a long and adventurous life, transforming from a hesitant hobbit into a courageous adventurer. His greatest adventures are chronicled in The Hobbit, where he not only faced dragons and goblins but also discovered the depths of his own bravery and resilience.
